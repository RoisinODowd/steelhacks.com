FEB 24TH & 25TH, 2018 - PITTSBURGH, PENNSYLVANIA

SteelHacks is an event hosted at the University of Pittsburgh, through the Department of Computer Science, for students at colleges & universities across the US who are passionate about getting their hands dirty using technology and design.

This year is the fourth annual Steelhacks hackathon. Each year students from around the Pittsburgh area and beyond come together to hack and make cool stuff.
