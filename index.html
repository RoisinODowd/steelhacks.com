FEB 19TH - 21TH, 2016 - PITTSBURGH, PENNSYLVANIA

SteelHacks is an MLH sanctioned hackathon hosted at the University of Pittsburgh for students at colleges & universities across the US who are passionate about getting their hands dirty using technology and design.

Last year was the first incarnation of SteelHacks. Students from around the Pittsburgh region came together to make cool stuff for the sake of making cool stuff.
