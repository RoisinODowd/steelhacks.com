Steel Hacks

March 1st & 2nd, 2019

Pittsburgh Pennsylvania

assignment_turned_inRegister

SteelHacks is an event hosted at the University of Pittsburgh, through the School of Computing and Information, for students at colleges & universities across the US who are passionate about getting their hands dirty using technology and design.

This year is the fifth annual Steelhacks hackathon. Each year students from around the Pittsburgh area and beyond come together to hack and make cool stuff.

question_answerFrequently Asked Questions

Who can attend SteelHacks?

Any undergraduate or graduate student at any college in the world! Non-Pitt college undergraduate students must be at least 18 years old. Unfortunately, high school students can't attend this year.

Does this cost money?

Not at all! In fact, we pay for all your meals and provide you with all the hardware and help you need at any time for free.

Where will SteelHacks be held?

SteelHacks 2019 will occur at the Alumni Hall on Pitt's campus, 4227 Fifth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15260

What is the code of conduct for this event?

All participants of SteelHacks 2019 will be required to follow the MLH code of conduct that can be found here.

What is my registration used for?

We participate in Major League Hacking (MLH) as a MLH Member Event. You authorize us to share certain application/registration information for event administration, ranking, MLH administration, and occasional messages about hackathons in line with the MLH Privacy Policy.

Will hardware be available?

Yes! There will be hardware available to use at the event. The inventory list will be out soon.

I can't register anymore!

Don't panic! Shoot an email to steelhacks@pitt.edu and we'll try to sort it out.

Where will I sleep?

If you're a Pitt student, you can sleep in your residence hall/apartment. For non-Pitt students, the venue will have designated sleeping places.

I missed the confirmation dealine! Can I still come?

Send us an email to steelhacks@pitt.edu, and we'll see if we can find you a spot!

How do I get there?

If you're coming from out-of-state, we unfortunately cannot offer any travel reimbursement at this time. Students at any university in Pittsburgh can simply take a Port Authority bus straight to the event, so that's simple.

How do teams work?

Teams can consist of 1-4 people!

What if FAQ didn't answer my question?

You can tweet it at us @SteelHacks, or email us at steelhacks@pitt.edu!
